About DeBugger

Founded in 2015, DeBugger has been solving pest problems with the precision and dedication of a software engineer debugging code. Our founder, a former programmer turned exterminator, realized that both bugs in code and bugs in homes require systematic problem-solving, attention to detail, and a commitment to finding the root cause.

Our Mission

To eliminate pests from your home and business with the same thoroughness and precision that a developer uses to debug software. We don't just treat symptoms—we find and fix the root cause of your pest problems.

Our Values

We believe in honesty, transparency, and results. Just like clean code, we deliver clean solutions. No shortcuts, no excuses—just effective pest control that works.

Our Team

Our technicians are certified, licensed, and continuously trained in the latest pest control methods. They're not just exterminators—they're bug detectives who track down every last pest.

Our Story

DeBugger was born from a simple observation: pest control and software debugging share more in common than you might think. Both require systematic thinking, identifying root causes, and implementing effective solutions. Our founder, after years of debugging code, decided to apply the same problem-solving mindset to a different kind of bug—the kind that crawls, flies, and invades your home.

What started as a small local business has grown into a trusted name in extermination services. We've helped thousands of homeowners and businesses eliminate their pest problems, always with the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence that defines great software engineering.

Today, DeBugger continues to innovate in pest control, using the latest technology and methods to keep your spaces bug-free. We're not just exterminators—we're problem solvers who happen to specialize in bugs.
